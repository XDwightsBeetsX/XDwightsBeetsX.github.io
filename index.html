The Worlds Toughest Canoe Race, The Texas Water Safari

Please check out YETI's "Shut Up and Paddle" video!

Team In Deep Ship finished the 2019 TWS in 58:12m, and were looking forward to setting a new best in 2020 until the coronavirus pandemic delayed the race until it was eventually cancelled. The race is 260 miles and the fastest teams finish in about 40 hours, depending on the year's water level and conditions.

John Bugge, a TWS legend, is well known for his racing canoes and we used his boats both years, putting them to the test as we scraped our way down the San Marcos and Guadalupe rivers in over 300 miles of training each year.

Despite surviving the grueling training runs, while portaging a dam early in the race one of our paddlers tore tendons in his knee and was forced to drop out, cheering the team on from our supply crew.
